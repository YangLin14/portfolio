As a Robotics Instructor, I provided comprehensive, hands-on STEM education to students by guiding them through robot assembly, visual coding, 3D modeling, and the fundamentals of artificial intelligence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Robotics Assembly: Instructed students in assembling OLLO and VEX GO robots, enhancing their technical skills and fostering teamwork.
  • Visual Coding: Taught coding fundamentals using Scratch, helping students create their own games to learn key concepts in an engaging way.
  • 3D Modeling & Printing: Guided students in creating 3D models with TinkerCAD and managed the 3D printing process to bring their digital creations to life.
  • AI Introduction: Familiarized students with modern AI technologies like ChatGPT, Suno, and AI image generators to keep them abreast of advancing trends.
