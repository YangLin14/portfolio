Flashy 🤖

An AI-driven platform that generates high-quality, interactive flashcards to promote critical thinking and enhance learning.

Project Summary 📝

Flashy is an innovative platform where you can create high-quality flashcards with ease—either manually or with the assistance of AI. The AI generates up to 12 thought-provoking questions and detailed answers at a time, creating an interactive learning experience that promotes critical thinking. With secure authentication via Clerk, users can save, edit, and organize an unlimited number of flashcards, all stored safely in Firebase.
