Joystick Controlled Car 🚗

A 3D-printed, Arduino-powered car with full directional control via a custom joystick controller.

Project Summary 📝

As vice president of the Ohlone IEEE club, I led a team to design and create an Arduino joystick-controlled car. We used 3D printing to fabricate the car's body and controller, programmed the logic in C for an Arduino Nano, and completed the full hardware assembly. We successfully showcased the final product to students and faculty at the school's career fair.

Features ✨

Core Functionality ⚙️

  • 🕹️ Full directional control via joystick
  • 🤖 Programmed using Arduino (C/C++)
  • 🛠️ Custom hardware assembly and wiring
  • 🔩 Custom-designed car body
