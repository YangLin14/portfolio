Movable Hand 🖐️

A 3D-printed, articulated hand designed in Fusion 360 to serve as a custom phone holder or an extra hand for small objects.

Project Summary 📝

This project was inspired by a simple desire: to watch anime on my phone without having to hold it. Instead of buying a boring phone holder, I decided to design my own. As a beginner learning CAD and 3D printing, this was a fantastic opportunity to gain new engineering skills. The result is a fun and functional movable hand that can hold a phone or other small items.

Core Functionality ⚙️
