NewLedger 💸

A modern expense tracking and financial management application built with Flutter, helping users manage their personal and shared expenses.

Project Summary 📝

NewLedger is a comprehensive financial management solution that combines intuitive expense tracking with powerful collaboration features. Built with Flutter and leveraging modern technologies like Firebase and ML Kit, the app offers a seamless cross-platform experience for both iOS and Android users. The goal was to create an app that makes it easy to answer "How much did I spend on my trip?" without the hassle of manual tracking.

Features ✨

Core Functionality ⚙️

  • 📊 Track personal and shared expenses
  • 🤝 Split expenses with friends and family
  • 📈 Detailed expense analytics and reporting
  • 🧾 Receipt scanning and storage via ML Kit
  • 📱 Cross-platform support (iOS, Android)

User Experience 🎨

  • 🌓 Dark/Light theme support
  • ✨ Modern, intuitive interface
  • 🎨 Visual expense breakdowns
  • 🔍 Advanced expense filtering
  • 🌍 Multi-currency support
