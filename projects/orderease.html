OrderEase App 🍔

A Flutter app to help non-native English speakers order food confidently by generating language-correct ordering sentences.

Project Summary 📝

Developed during my internship at the Frugal Innovation Hub, this app was inspired by my own experiences as an immigrant from Taiwan. The fear and discomfort of ordering food in a non-native language motivated me to create a tool to empower others in similar situations. The app provides real-time language translation and uses simple images to convey information. It allows users to browse menus, select meals, and generate correct ordering sentences, with an audio feature to help communicate special requests.
