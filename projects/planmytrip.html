PlanMyTrip ✈️

An AI-powered chatbot designed to make travel planning effortless with personalized itineraries, checklists, and tips.

Project Summary 📝

PlanMyTrip is an intuitive, AI-powered platform designed to make travel planning effortless. Its intelligent chatbot guides users every step of the way, offering personalized recommendations, ideal itineraries, packing checklists, and essential travel tips. The goal is to streamline logistics so you can focus on enjoying your journey.
